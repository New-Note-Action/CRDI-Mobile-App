To Use This App

  1. Tap "New Test" on the main menu.
  2. From the "Select Profile" dropdown menu that follows, select the profile that you are to use as told by your teacher or proctor.
  3. At the next screen, tap "Start" once the media or activity you will be responding to begins.
  4. You will then be at the testing screen. Manipulate the controls (explained below) as described in the text that appears above the controls.
